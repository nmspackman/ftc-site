Order Up! is a customizable solution for every Food Truck's cashiering needs. This is the future of fast-food.

    We aim to:
  • Decrease the costs of additional employees
  • Eliminate clerical error
  • Increase throughput at the POS
  • Free up time for the owners to focus on the food and the customers
    Our app solves this by:
  • Enabling the customers to build orders and complete transactions
  • Allowing the managers to dynamically build and manage their menus
